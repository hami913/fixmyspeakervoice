Sound and Vibration: How They Work Together

It is necessary to understand that sound (a form of energy made by vibrations) and vibration(To and fro or back and forth motion of an object or particles in a medium) have different roles. But in Fix, my speaker, these two things work together, proving beneficial. We explain sound and vibration in two different terms to make you understand. I will also tell you how they prove to be beneficiaries together.

Sound Waves:

Sound waves are longitudinal waves that travel through a medium like air or water. Playing a low-frequency sound, like a 165Hz tone(ideal), through your speaker creates sound waves that make the speaker’s diaphragm. (The thin, semi-rigid membrane attached to the voice coil) move back and forth. This movement creates vibrations inside the speaker, which help shake loose any water or dust blocking the sound.

Vibrations:

When an object vibrates, it generates movement, which is strong enough to dislodge and expel water droplets, dust, or small particles from the speaker. As a result, these blockages are cleared, allowing the sound to reemerge and enhancing the speaker’s performance.

NOTE: In simple terms, sound waves create vibrations, which help clear out anything stopping your speaker from working correctly.

Keeping your phone speaker clean and dry is important for its performance and longevity. Water damage can harm the hardware, but you can reduce these risks with effective methods.
